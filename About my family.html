About my family

The smart official family is one of the families in the world that i'm ever proud to be. My family is not too large or consist of many people because, it's just a few members. Though we are so small in numbers, we are also good at our own managements.We are just four in numbers in my small nuclear family.My father the head of the house,My mother who is the second in command and care taker of the home,My younger sister,and Myself who are the erran runners of the house and the pride of the home.
   Our family is involed in Agricultural practices. We cultivate, we harvest, and also sells.
Our family also practises the two type of Agriculture namely;

  • Subsistence Agriculture
  • Commercial Agriculture

Subsistence Agriculture: This is the type of Agriculture whereby the farmer cultivates crops for himself and his family's consumption alone.
Commercial Agriculture: This is the type of Agriculture whereby the farmer cultivates crops for sale.
