About My Father

My father the head of the family and also the bread winner of the family.He is Mr. Williams. He super sees the affair of the house,and work hard to see that there is food on the family's table.
   My father is an Ederly man who hails from Ediode village. He is so respected most especially in our family. My father has his own farmland seprately from the general family farmland we work on. He uses that for commercial purposes. In that farm he grows Cassava,and Yams mainly. He rearly grows maize.

We love my father just as my father loves us all. We pray that may the almight God continue to keep him for us,and we for him. Amen.
